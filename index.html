Who we are

We are a 56-strong all male acapella (unaccompanied) harmony chorus based in Taunton, Somerset. We meet every Thursday evening at the Bishop Henderson School, in Taunton between 7pm and 10pm.

Our aim is to encourage the enjoyment of singing through a continuous high quality educational barbershop singing programme. We are the only chorus of it's type in Somerset
